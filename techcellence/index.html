TECHcellence

4,575 students applied to the Computer Science program in 2021, with only 340 enrolling. The program notes that local and first-generation students faced admission barriers.

Program Components

Summer Academy

The initiative targets a cohort of 90 low-income, first generation high school students through a summer program featuring introductory computer science coursework, faculty seminars, and mentoring activities.

Curricula Alignment

Faculty from MUSD, SJCC, and SJSU will align curricula and articulate requirements across institutions to enable dual enrollment opportunities reducing college costs.

Culturally Sustaining Mentorship

Provides cohort-based mentoring from SJSU undergraduate CS majors and aspiring K12 CS teachers with training in culturally sustaining educational practices.

Partnership

The program involves:

  • Milpitas Unified School District
  • San José City College
  • San José State University

Supported by an APLU grant and California K-12 Strong Workforce funding.
