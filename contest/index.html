SJSU Local Programming Contest

Note: This page contains information from the 2022 contest. Check back for updates on future contests.

Contest Details (2022)

The SJSU local programming competition takes place at MacQuarrie Hall.

  • Date: April 30th, 2022
  • Time: 11:00 AM - 3:00 PM
  • Location: MacQuarrie Hall, SJSU
  • Platform: Kattis

Eligibility

Participation is open to:

  • SJSU undergraduates
  • Admitted students
  • Local community college invitees
  • SJSU faculty

Eligibility determined by ICPC decision tree.

Team Requirements

  • Teams consist of exactly 3 members
  • ICPC-style competition format
  • Participants must practice on open.kattis.com beforehand
  • Only API documentation permitted during competition
  • No pre-written code allowed

Contest Rules

  1. Three pre-registered eligible members required
  2. No external resources allowed except language/API references
  3. Kattis judges solutions (final authority)
  4. Winning teams must explain their chosen solution
  5. All team members must attend opening Zoom call and closing ceremony

Schedule

  • 11:00 AM: Teams must join opening Zoom call
  • 11:30 AM: Competition begins
  • 3:00 PM: Results announced; winners explain solutions

Prizes

Three winning teams receive cash awards of $150 each (split three ways):

  • 1st place team
  • Highest-ranked SJSU team
  • Highest-ranked team with admitted or first-year student
