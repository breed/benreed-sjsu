SJSU Local Contest 2022

we are pleased to invite you to participate in the 2022 SJSU local programming contest. it will be taking place in MacQuarrie Hall on april 30th, 2022 at 11AM until 3PM.

we invite SJSU undergraduates, admitted students, local community college students who have received a special invitation, and SJSU faculty to put together teams.

Team Requirements

  • Teams consist of exactly 3 members
  • ICPC-style competition format
  • contestants work in teams of 3
  • Contest runs on Kattis platform
  • Participants must practice on open.kattis.com beforehand
  • Only API documentation permitted during competition
  • Eligibility determined by ICPC decision tree
  • YOU MUST BE REGISTERED BY MIDNIGHT APRIL 29th

Contest Rules

  1. Three pre-registered eligible members required
  2. teams cannot use any outside resources including people, notes, books, google, etc except for language and API references
  3. no prewritten code or public code may be used
  4. Kattis judges solutions (final authority)
  5. Winning teams must explain a solution chosen by contest coordinator/audience
  6. All team members must attend opening Zoom call and closing ceremony

Contest Schedule

  • 11:00 AM: Teams must join opening Zoom call
  • 11:30 AM: Competition begins (3-hour contest)
  • 3:00 PM: Results announced; winners explain solutions

Prizes

each winning team will receive $150 to split 3 ways

Three categories:

  • 1st place team
  • Highest SJSU team
  • Highest team with admitted or first-year student
