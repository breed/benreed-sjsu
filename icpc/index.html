SJSU ICPC Programming Team

The ICPC (International Collegiate Programming Competition) is a very old world-wide programming competition for undergraduates. I participated when I was an undergraduate at Miami University, and now I coach the SJSU teams.

Team Years

2025 2022 2021 2020 2019

Team Composition

  • Each team consists of 3 members
  • Up to 5 teams can compete regionally
  • Your major is NOT a factor in eligibility
  • Freshmen and sophomores are especially encouraged to participate

Preparation

  • Registration required on Kattis (open.kattis.com)
  • Weekly solution sessions led by student trainers
  • Friday/Saturday 3-5 hour practice competitions
  • Grades are prioritized over practice attendance

Resources

  • SJSU Local Contest
  • SJSU Competitive Programming Discord
